Pancake Recipe

Fluffy and delicious pancakes perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Chef's Tips

Don't overmix the batter; lumps are okay. Use a non-stick pan for perfect pancakes.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry and stir gently until combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and grease lightly.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Cook other side until golden brown. Serve warm with syrup or toppings.
